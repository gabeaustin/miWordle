miWordle
Guess the wordle in six tries. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit. After each guess, the color of the ties will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
Examples
WEARY
The letter W is in the word and in the correct spot.
PILLS
The letter I is in the word but in the wrong spot.
VAGUE
The letter U is not in the word in any spot.
